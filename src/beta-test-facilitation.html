Facilitation

Beta testing is an essential part of our iterative product design process. We look to you, our Authorized Partners, and your clients for your active participation and feedback to help us discover what works with our products, what doesnâ€™t, and why.

Concept Tests Beta Tests
  • For Partners
  • Occur at start of development process
  • A small group of people participates
  • Offer a chance to provide high-level feedback about the merits of a product idea or prototype
  • Depending on feedback, concept tests may not lead to a beta version.
  • For Partners and clients
  • May occur multiple times throughout development process
  • Many people can participate
  • Offer a chance to provide experiential feedback as you "test drive" the products in classroom trials or real-world applications
  • Depending on feedback, beta tests may not lead to a launched product.
